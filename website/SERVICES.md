# Services

This page describes public-facing control work and review contracts, not runtime
ownership or final artifact acceptance.

## Offer 1: AI Project Control Diagnostic

Timeline:

```text
1-2 weeks
```

Best for:

- non-engineer founders;
- creators with AI product ideas;
- teams with too many demos and no route;
- operators who need to decide what to do before hiring or building.

Scope:

- clarify original intent;
- map sources and gaps;
- identify candidate tool/agent route;
- name owner truth boundaries;
- design the first safe task packet;
- define review checklist and next evidence.

Output:

- intent map;
- source/tool route;
- risk boundary;
- first task packet;
- review checklist;
- outcome-delta note.

Not in scope:

- writing full production code;
- accepting final product truth;
- managing billing/provider accounts;
- guaranteeing market success.

## Offer 3: Workflow Rescue Review

Timeline:

```text
1-3 weeks
```

Best for:

- projects with many drafts, prompts, repos, screenshots, or demos;
- teams that do not know what is current;
- operators who need a keep/kill map.

Scope:

- reconstruct current state;
- separate source, candidate, fixture, report, and generated artifact layers;
- identify overclaims;
- decide what to keep, kill, rewrite, or review;
- create the next execution route.

Output:

- keep/kill map;
- current-state note;
- evidence ladder;
- next-action packet;
- public-safe lessons if allowed.

Not in scope:

- destructive cleanup without explicit approval;
- moving or deleting private assets;
- claiming acceptance on behalf of project owners.
