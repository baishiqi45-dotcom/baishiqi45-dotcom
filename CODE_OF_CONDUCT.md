# Code Of Conduct

This repository is a public-safe method library. Participation should help
people discuss AI video production control, Project Clinic, workflow rescue,
and artifact verification without exposing private material.

## Expected Behavior

- Be direct, specific, and respectful.
- Keep examples synthetic, self-owned, or explicitly public.
- Critique methods and wording, not people.
- Name unsupported claims, privacy risks, and acceptance-boundary problems
  clearly.
- Use issue templates and pull requests for focused, reviewable changes.

## Not Accepted

- Harassment, personal attacks, slurs, or intimidation.
- Private scripts, raw media, provider/account state, customer material, local
  workspace paths, credentials, or final acceptance evidence.
- Bought stars, fake engagement, spam, mass promotion, or manipulative growth
  tactics.
- Claims that synthetic examples prove client outcomes, provider reliability,
  production readiness, commercial clearance, or final artifact acceptance.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, close, delete, or block issues, pull requests, and
comments that violate these rules or put private material at risk.
